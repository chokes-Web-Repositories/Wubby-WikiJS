## {.tabset}
### ;fly (player)
-----

when said in chat, it will enable you to fly.

### ;kill (player)
-----

when said in chat, it kills the (player).

### ;hub (player)
----

when said in chat, it hubs the (player)

### ;speed (player) (number)
----

when said in chat, it will set the (player) speed to (number).
